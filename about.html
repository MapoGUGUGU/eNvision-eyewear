Our Origin

A Vision Born in a
Small Workshop

eNVision was founded in 1873 in a modest workshop in Jakarta's Kebun Jeruk district, where master optician Sir Archduke Cyrus Le Phoque of Anatolia spent his evenings crafting custom frames for clients who felt underserved by the mass-produced eyewear market.

His conviction was simple but radical: eyewear is not a commodity, it is an intimate accessory, worn every waking hour, that should be chosen with the same deliberate care as a fine watch or tailored suit. This belief attracted the attention of a legendary designer Maximus Alexander, whose background in industrial design brought a new dimension to the workshop's offerings.

Together, they formalized eNVision Optics in 1873, combining clinical expertise with design thinking to create a range of frames that were as technically precise as they were aesthetically distinguished. The rest, as our clients say, is clarity.

What Drives Us

Our Philosophy

Three principles guide every decision we make, from the materials we source to the way we serve each client.

⬡

Uncompromising Design

Every frame begins as a sketch, progresses through prototyping, and is refined through multiple iterations before it earns the eNVision name. We believe that great design is in the details that most people never notice but always feel.

◈

Optical Precision

Our frames are engineered to the exact tolerances required for precise optical alignment. A beautiful frame that distorts your vision is a failure. We ensure that aesthetics and functionality are never in conflict, they are inseparable.

◯

Client-First Service

We measure our success not in frames sold but in lives improved. Each client receives personalised attention from our team from initial consultation through fitting, adjustments, and beyond. Your satisfaction is our enduring commitment.

The Team Behind the Vision

Meet Our Founders

Three visionaries united by a shared passion for optical excellence and design integrity.

Sir Archduke Cyrus Le Phoque of Anatolia

Co-Founder & Chief Optician

A Fellow of the Bologna College of Optometrists with over 97 years of clinical experience, He leads our optical team and sets the standard for precision in every frame we produce. His work has been recognised by the International Optical Association. Sadly his photograph was lost in a fire, but his legacy lives on in every pair of eNVision frames.

Maximus Alexander

Co-Founder & Creative Director

A graduate of the Royal College of Art with a focus on product design, Maximus brings a deeply considered design philosophy to every eNVision collection. His frames have been exhibited at design weeks in Milan, Tokyo, and Singapore.

Augustine Bernadette

Co-Founder & Head of Client Relations

With a background in luxury hospitality and retail management, Augustine ensures that every client touchpoint at eNVision reflects the warmth, discretion, and care that defines a truly premium service experience.
